This repository serves as a centralized and open collection point for **Geographic Information System (GIS)** data related to the island of **Corfu (Kerkyra), Greece**. 

> [!IMPORTANT]
> The datasets provided in this repository are for informational purposes and public use. Always verify the accuracy of the spatial data before using it in critical infrastructure or official planning projects.

Our primary goal is to provide structured, clean, and easily accessible datasets that support advanced spatial analysis, urban planning, environmental monitoring, and custom map applications. The datasets focus heavily on transportation networks, urban infrastructure, environmental zones, and key geographical points of interest.

## 📂 Repository Structure

The data is organized into thematic folders, each containing specific subcategories of geospatial information. Below is an overview of the structure:

* `📁 transportation/`: Datasets related to transport networks and mobility.
  * `📁 traffic_lights/`: Spatial data and coordinates for traffic light locations.
  * `📁 road_network/`: Polyline data for main roads, secondary streets, and pathways.
  * `📁 public_transit/`: Point data for bus stops and polyline routes.
* `📁 infrastructure/`: Public utilities and essential facilities.
  * `📁 buildings/`: Footprints and attributes of residential and commercial buildings.
  * `📁 health/`: Locations of hospitals, clinics, and pharmacies.
* `📁 environment/`: Natural resources, parks, and protected areas.
  * `📁 parks/`: Polygons representing urban parks and green spaces.
  * `📁 water_bodies/`: Lakes, rivers, and coastal lines.
